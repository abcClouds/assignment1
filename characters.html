Characters

Lilian Kemp

The main protagonist, a kind and charming girl who goes to Fraleric by herself.

Vilem Schuyler

A soft-spoken boy who seems to stay away from people. He loves animals, especially horses, but they always run away from him.

Karessa Outterridge

The elegant princess of Aeranyth with a heart rooted to theater. She seems to be detached to her older brother, the king.

Paxton Lowell

A hot-headed boy running from home. He doesn't talk about where he came from or his family.

Mindie Fairburn

A savvy girl who knows a bit more than she probably should. This may cause her downfall...
